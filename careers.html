Mechanical Engineer: Researches, plans and develops mechanical and electromechanical products and systems. Coordinates and oversees activities involved in fabrication, operation, application and installation and repair of mechanical and electromechanical systems and products.
Examines and analyzes customer design requirements, specifications and manuals to ascertain feasibility of designs and applications. Advises and coordinates fabrication and installation to ensure products and systems conform to engineering principles and customer specifications. Identify and prioritize potential actions for immediate cost reduction.

Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering is required for this position. Job Location/Resume to: SSR MFG Corporation – 1834 Ferguson Lane, Suite 600, Austin, Texas 78754.
